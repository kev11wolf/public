💰  Entry:  $20 per participant — Venmo @Evan-Sommerville once registration opens.

⚠️  Tiger Won’t Be at Augusta This Year

Following his DUI arrest last week and a statement released Tuesday, Tiger Woods confirmed he is stepping away from golf “for a period of time” to seek treatment and focus on his health. Augusta National chairman Fred Ridley confirmed the five-time Masters champion will not be in the field for 2026 — a notable absence heading into what should be a wide-open week at Augusta. We wish Tiger well as he takes the time he needs.

 

🍽  Rory’s Champions Dinner Menu — Tuesday, April 7

Defending champion Rory McIlroy hosts the annual Champions Dinner for all past Masters winners on Tuesday night. His menu blends Northern Irish comfort food with Augusta tradition and a few luxurious touches:
