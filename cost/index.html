Engine Assumptions & Logic

1. Inflation Scaling: To prevent your long-term model from breaking, Income, Taxes, HSA/401k Contributions, and Living Expenses automatically grow by your targeted Inflation Rate every year.

2. Escrow & Home Scaling: Your Home Value appreciates at your specified rate. Because property taxes and insurance scale with home value, your Mortgage Escrow outflow dynamically grows at this exact same appreciation rate over time.

3. Fixed Debt: Mortgage P&I, Auto Loans, and Student Loans remain completely flat against inflation. Once their balances hit $0, their monthly payments are swept directly into your Free Cash Flow / Savings bucket.

4. Vehicle Depreciation: Your vehicle value drops annually by the targeted percentage, stopping at 0.

5. Real vs. Nominal: Nominal means the exact bank balance. Real means discounting that bank balance back to today's purchasing power using the inflation rate.
